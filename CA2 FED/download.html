Required specs

Software requirements

  • Windows 10 (32/64-bit)
  • Windows 8.1 (32/64-bit)
  • Windows 8 (32/64-bit)
  • Windows 7 (32/64-bit)
  • Active Internet connection

Hardware requirements

  • 800MHz CPU or faster, with SSE2 technology
  • 2048 MB (64-bit OS), 1024 MB (32-bit OS)
  • 250 MB of free hard disk space
  • 1024x768 or higher screen resolution

Languages supported

    English, French, German, Chinese (Simplified and Traditional), Spanish, Italian, Russian
