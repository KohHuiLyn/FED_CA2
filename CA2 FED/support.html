FAQs

  • Is CleanMan safe to use?
  • Yes! CleanMan is safe to use and will provide 24/7 coverage of your device.
  • Is CleanMan free?
  • Yes! CleanMan is, and always will be free. However, if you like our product and would like to help us, consider donating to us at our Download Page.
  • My CleanMan isn't working.
  • Have you tried turning your computer off and on again? Just kidding! Try checking your settings in your computer, and make sure that CleanMan is allowed in your firewall. If that doesn't work, please contact our friendly staff here.
  • How do I disable CleanMan from popping up when my PC starts?
  • Simply go to your settings, and either check "Run CleanMan in background on start-up" or "Disable run on start-up" if you would like CleanMan to not run in the background.

Protection Tips

Suspicious Links

Too good to be true? It probably is. Don't click on suspicious links that tempt you with offers like "Click here for a free IPad!". Clicking on these links can result in malware being downloaded on your computer and your personal information being leaked!

Suspicious Emails

Have a suspicious email in your inbox accusing you of having some payments overdue? Don't know what it is talking about? Don't be scared. These scam emails are extremely common, but harmless. They are best left ignored. Unless -that is- you do have unpaid bills.

Online Transactions

When dealing with an online buyer, always check that they are reliable. Many scam shopping sites/sellers exist. Look through customer reviews, their sold goods, and item reviews and verify that this seller is safe and will deliver.
